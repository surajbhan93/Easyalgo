How to Start a Career in EdTech?

If you're passionate about education and technology, a career in EdTech might be right for you. Here are some steps to get started:

Educational Background

Obtain a degree in education, computer science, instructional design, or related fields.

Gain Skills

Learn about educational technology tools, instructional design principles, and project management.

Build Experience

Gain experience through internships, freelance projects, or volunteering in educational settings.

Networking

Connect with professionals in the EdTech industry through events, conferences, and online communities.
